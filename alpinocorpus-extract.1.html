NAME

alpinocorpus-extract -- Extract Alpino treebank entry to a directory

SYNOPSIS

alpinocorpus-extract treebank directory

DESCRIPTION

The alpinocorpus-extract utility extracts a treebank to a directory. The treebank can either be a Dact treebank or a compact corpus (.index or .data.dz file).
